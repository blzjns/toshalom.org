Welcome to a vibrant celebration where faith meets adventure, and hearts are set ablaze with the fire of God’s love. ACAMPS North Festival, hosted annually in the scenic embrace of New England, beckons young adults from across the USA and Canada to embark on a journey of faith, community, and profound personal discovery.

An Unforgettable Experience

ACAMPS isn’t just an event—it’s a transformative experience designed to resonate deeply with young hearts seeking purpose and connection. Rooted in the spirituality of the Shalom Catholic Community, ACAMPS blends dynamic worship, engaging talks, and pulsating music to create an atmosphere where faith comes alive and friendships flourish.

A Fusion of Faith and Fun

Imagine spending days surrounded by the majestic beauty of New England’s landscapes, where the rustling leaves whisper ancient wisdom and the crisp air carries the promise of new beginnings. At ACAMPS, participants dive into inspiring workshops, share authentic moments of prayer and reflection, and forge bonds that transcend the ordinary.

Nurturing Community Spirit

Central to ACAMPS is the spirit of community and camaraderie that permeates every aspect of the festival. Whether gathered around a bonfire, sharing stories of faith and personal growth, or engaging in passionate discussions about living out Catholic values in today’s world, young adults discover a sense of belonging that fuels their journey of faith.

Empowering Young Leaders

ACAMPS empowers young adults to embrace leadership roles within their communities, equipping them with tools to make a meaningful impact in society. Through workshops focused on social justice, evangelization, and personal growth, participants are inspired to become catalysts for positive change in their local contexts.

Celebrating Diversity and Unity

In the spirit of the Shalom Catholic Community, ACAMPS celebrates the diversity of God’s creation. Young adults from various cultural backgrounds and life experiences come together to celebrate their shared faith, fostering a deep sense of unity amidst diversity and strengthening their commitment to living as disciples of Christ.

Join the Journey

If you’re a young adult hungry for an authentic encounter with God and eager to connect with peers who share your passion for faith, ACAMPS awaits. Step into a weekend that promises not only spiritual rejuvenation but also the opportunity to build lasting friendships and inspire one another to live boldly for Christ.
